Fun Run/Walk

Join us early on Cultural Day for an optional 5k fun run and walk beginning at 8:00am. We will meet at Salmon Street Springs Park at 7:45, with the Run/Walk beginning at 8 am. For more information visit: https://kohacon2018.bywatersolutions.com/funrun.html
