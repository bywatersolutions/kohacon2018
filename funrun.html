Fun Run

September 13 at 8:00am
5K Run and Walk

Join us early on Cultural Day (Thursday, 9/13) for a Fun Run/Walk (approximately 5K) along the banks of the Willamette River in Portland. All are encouraged to participate, as this is not a race but an opportunity to start the day running!

Location: We will meet at Salmon Street Springs Park at 7:45, with the Run/Walk beginning at 8 am.

Turn by Turn Directions

  • Start at Salmon Street Springs Park
  • Follow the Waterfront Park Trail heading North
  • Cross the river at the N Steel Bridge
  • Connect to the Eastbank Esplanade, heading South
  • Cross the river again at the SW Hawthorne Bridge
  • Loop through Tom McCall Waterfront Park
  • Return to Salmon Street Springs

Water will be provided.

If you plan on participating, please contact adam@bywatersolutions.com so we have an accurate headcount.
